## Lemur Anatomy & Biology
* Average Height - 70cm
* Average Weight - 9kg
* Physical Characteristics - Ring tail, grey fur, white tufts
* Lemurs are land mammals and breath through their lungs
* Lemurs live an average of 16 - 19 years
